League of Legends Match History

I play a popular game called League of Legends by Riot Games. There are many websites that utilize Riot Games API to display your match history.

I have tried to look for a website that allows me to filter my match history in more depth than just the champion I'm playing, but I have yet to find one

So I decided I'm going to make one! This will allow me to filter my match history based on my champion, my keystone rune, the items I buy in game, and possibly who I am fighting against in my lane. I say possibly because this project is still in the works, but I'm really excited to see what I can make it do!
